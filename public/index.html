The Conference

We are excited to announce the 2nd Veterinary Ophthalmic Surgery Meeting. This long awaited event will be an innovative meeting, with a dynamic format developed to encourage a candid debate among Veterinary Ophthalmologists with interests in Surgery.

This is a limited-space event that will take place on Aug 3-5th, 2018 in Chicago, IL.

Come be part of an interactive gathering that will challenge and inspire young professionals and engage the most renowned clinicians with compelling case discussions, pertinent peals, updates from human meetings, and also a Keynote Lecture!
